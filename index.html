Driving the Next Generation of Fintech Innovation

Turning AI and No-Code into Solutions that Deliver Impact

I'm a passionate product builder and fintech innovator on a mission to transform how people across Africa and the diaspora send, spend, and support their communities. My work sits at the intersection of financial technology and user experience. The goal is to help bring financial services to life in ways that are accessible and impactful.

Over the years, I've led and contributed to 50+ projects and collaborated with 15+ financial institutions and tech partners, turning ideas into market-ready solutions. Whether it's through mobile money, remittances, digital banking or user-first product development, my focus is always on delivering measurable impact.

Current Ventures

  • Indelible – A powerful crowdfunding platform that is transforming nature-finance by connecting businesses, institutions and individuals to high-impact biodiversity projects.
  • VibeCode Africa – A growing digital ecosystem that is fueling Africa's tech talent with AI and no-code tools to make software creation accessible to all.
  • Cend – A groundbreaking fintech app. More details to be shared later.

My work blends deep fintech expertise with experience working alongside major banks, telecoms, and startups. I specialize in mobile money, agent networks, digital payments, and scalable product design for underserved markets.
